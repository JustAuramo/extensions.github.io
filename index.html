Why does Trakt Scrobbler exist?

This extension uses the trakt.tv api key, everything is ready and all you have to do is log in to your account. don't worry. logging in has nothing to do with anything other than saving your history to the right account

I thought I needed a feature to transfer my history to the trakt.tv service, where I can keep track of what I've watched and when.

Scrobbler features

The API key provides us with these features:

  • Save your viewing history on trakt.tv
  • You can rate the content and write a comment if you wish.
  • You can share on social media what you have recently watched.
  • The service works on many sites.
